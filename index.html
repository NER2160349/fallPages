Why Fall?

Fall is the best season of the year, with leaves in beautiful hues, crisp air, perfect weather and a pelthora of holidays, there is always something to enjoy during this mysterious and calming season.
Not falling for fall? This website provides a guide to through Fall! From books, history and places, you'll be able to get into the fall spirit in no time!
